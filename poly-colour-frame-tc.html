Poly-Colour Frame-Tc

Garments made from shiny nylon warp yarns and coloured polyester weft yarns. The different tensions of the weft and warp yarns create a three-dimensional effect, an embossed-looking square, with a metallic and iridescent appearance boosted by the garment dye procedure. Within outerwear pieces, the anorak is padded with the finest feathers, specifically treated to withstand the garment dyeing procedure, while the jacket features a PrimaLoft®-TC layer for superior thermal performance. The Poly-Colour Frame package also includes overshirts, fleecewear and trousers.
